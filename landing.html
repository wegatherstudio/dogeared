Inside the app

Everything a reader actually needs.

📖 Sessions, tracked like workouts

Hit start when you start reading. Dogeared times your session, tracks the pages you cover, and asks how it felt when you're done — then wraps it into a satisfying summary the moment you finish.

You pick up your book on the train. Two stops later, close the app and watch your streak and pages add up — no manual math required.

⏱️
✍️ A journal that lives on your homepage

No digging through tabs. Your two latest entries sit right on Home, ready to reread or edit. Capture a Reflection, a Character note, a bit of Analysis, or a Quote — each with its own prompts — and drop in up to four photos per entry.

A line hits you mid-chapter. You save it as a Quote entry with a photo of the page, right there, before the feeling fades.

📓
🔍 Discover, made for you

A full-screen, swipeable feed of books picked from what you actually like. Skip what's not for you — it's gone for good. Tap "deal me more" and it keeps surfacing fresh titles, live, so you're never stuck staring at the same five books.

You finish a book and have no idea what's next. Thirty seconds of swiping later, you do.

🔮
📚 A library that's actually yours

Every book you've read, are reading, or want to read — organized, searchable, and built from real book data. Not a wishlist you forget. A shelf you keep coming back to.

Open Profile any time and see, at a glance, the reading life you've actually built.

📚
🎨 Share cards worth posting

Turn any book or quote into a designed, story-ready card — a gradient pulled from the book's own cover, the classic Dogeared gold-to-ember look, or transparent for laying over your own photo. Every card you make is saved to your profile.

You just finished a book that wrecked you. One tap, and you've got something beautiful to share — not a screenshot of your notes app.

✨
🎉 Your Monthly Wrap

Once a month, Dogeared quietly puts together your own recap: what you read, your standout quote, how this month compares to last, and a gentle nudge for what's next.

End of the month, you get a little celebration of the reading you actually did — quiet months included.

🗓️
🏆 Progress that feels good, not gimmicky

Earn XP, level up, and unlock achievements as you read — no cartoon badges, no noise. Just quiet recognition that you're building something real.

A small streak counter that somehow makes you want to pick the book back up tonight.
